ABOUT US

Discover the story, mission, and values that drive Shaheen Academy School Scouts to build character and leadership through outdoor adventures.

OUR STORY

Shaheen Academy School Scouts was established in 1998 with a simple mission: to develop the next generation of leaders with strong values, resilience, and practical skills. What began as a small group of 15 students has grown into a vibrant community of over 200 scouts.

Our program has evolved over the years, but our commitment to character development, outdoor adventure, and community service has remained unwavering. As we've grown, we've expanded our programs to encompass a wider range of activities and learning opportunities.

Today, Shaheen Academy School Scouts continues to be a place where young people can discover their potential, build lifelong friendships, and develop the skills they need to become responsible citizens and leaders.

OUR MISSION & VALUES

Our Mission

To develop young people who are physically strong, mentally awake, and morally straight through outdoor activities, skill development, and community service.

We strive to create an environment where scouts can learn by doing, grow through challenges, and develop a lifelong love for nature and service to others.

Our Values

  • Character

    Integrity, honesty, and ethical decision-making in all actions.

  • Leadership

    Developing the ability to guide, inspire, and work effectively with others.

  • Service

    Commitment to helping others and making a positive difference in our community.

  • Outdoor Skills

    Appreciation for nature and ability to thrive in outdoor environments.

  • Teamwork

    Collaboration, communication, and respect for diverse perspectives.

WHAT WE DO

Outdoor Adventures

Regular camping trips, hiking expeditions, and nature exploration activities that build practical skills and resilience.

  • Weekend campouts
  • Mountain hiking expeditions
  • Wilderness survival training
  • Star-gazing and astronomy

Community Service

Volunteer projects that give back to our community and teach the importance of civic responsibility.

  • Environmental clean-up days
  • Food drives for local shelters
  • Tree planting initiatives
  • Support for elderly care homes

Skill Development

Structured badge programs and workshops to develop practical life skills and specialized knowledge.

  • First aid and emergency preparedness
  • Navigation and orienteering
  • Leadership and communication
  • Environmental conservation

OUR JOURNEY

1998

Founding

Shaheen Academy School Scouts was established with its first troop of 15 students and 2 dedicated leaders.

2005

Expansion

The program expanded to include three age groups and grew to over 50 members, introducing new activities and badges.

2012

Regional Recognition

Our troop received regional recognition for outstanding community service and environmental conservation efforts.

2018

20th Anniversary

Celebrated 20 years of impact with a special jamboree and launched the alumni network to maintain connections.

Present

A Thriving Community

Today, we are a vibrant community of over 200 scouts with a wide range of programs and a strong focus on leadership and environmental stewardship.
